About

I am an AI infrastructure engineer and systems researcher at Baidu, where I build efficient, scalable training systems and push the performance limits of large-model training. I lead LoongForge (formerly AIAK-Training-LLM), an open-source training framework for LLMs, VLMs, diffusion, and embodied models.

I received my MSc from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My work centers on bridging algorithmic needs with high-performance infrastructure.

Research Interests

  • Training infrastructure for large-scale models
  • Training acceleration & efficiency (parallelism, memory, throughput)
  • Systems optimization for LLM and VLM
  • Embodied intelligence

LoongForge
